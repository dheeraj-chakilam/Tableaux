Welcome to Constableaux!

This is a propositional logic solver that uses the Tableaux method to visualize proofs for you. It can also step through the proof to show you how exactly the result was derived.

To start, enter any propositional logic expression below. Negations are represented with ~, conjunctions with ^, disjunctions with v, and implications with ->. Parentheses are important, and please restrict propositional variable names to uppercase alphabets!
