Welcome to Constableaux!

This is a propositional logic solver that uses the Tableaux method to visualize proofs for you. It can also step through the proof to show you how exactly the result was derived.

To start, enter any propositional logic expression below. While writing the expression, keep in mind that:

  • Negations are represented with ~
  • Conjunctions are represented with ^
  • Disjunctions are represented with v
  • Implications are represented with ->
  • Parentheses are important for associativity!
  • Propositional variables should be represented as English alphabets (not case-sensitive)
  • Example expression: (~(A ^ B)) -> (~A v ~B)
